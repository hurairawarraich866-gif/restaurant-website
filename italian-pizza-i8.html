Tucked into Aneeq Arcade on Tipu Sultan Road, Italian Pizza has been I-8 Markaz's reliable answer to "let's just get pizza" since before the neighborhood had half its current restaurants.

No pretension, no Rs 3,000 truffle pies — just honest, hot, cheesy pizza made fast, served all day and through the night, for whoever's hungry and whenever they are.

It's the kind of place locals don't think twice about: reliably open, reliably cheap, reliably good enough that 347 people left a review.
